Biography

Derek Shepherd is Chief of Neurosurgery,as the lead male character, he is known for his complicated love life.He also earns the nickname "McDreamy" from the female staff of Seattle Grace.

Season Highlights

  • He initially moves to Seattle from New York City to get away from his ex-wife, Addison Montgomery, who he discovers is having an affair with his best friend, Mark Sloan.He subsequently begins a long romance with Meredith Grey, which is on and off until their marriage in Season 5.

Meredith then gave birth to their third child, a daughter named Ellis, who was likely conceived the day before he died.
